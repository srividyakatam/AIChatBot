Welcome to Prompt Engineering Chatbot Applications

This project is to demonstrate chatbot applications integrated with prompt engineering techniques.

The application involves the development of an advanced, multilingual chatbots. Utilizing OpenAI's GPT-3 for natural language processing, the chatbots are designed to handle various customer queries with different personalities and in multiple languages. The application is structured to be adaptable, allowing for the integration of additional contexts and features.

Customer Service Chatbot

Customer Service Chatbot involves persona pattern and zero-shot prompting technique. This is the basic form of prompt engineering technique to contextualize the GPT-3's knowledge.

Educational Tutor Chatbot

Educational Tutor Chatbot involves persona pattern and few-shot prompting technique. This is a bit refined form of prompt engineering technique to contextualize the GPT-3's knowledge while instructing the AI to respond in certain way.
