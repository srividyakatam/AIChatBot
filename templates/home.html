Welcome to Prompt Engineering Chatbot Applications

This project is to demonstrate chatbot applications integrated with prompt engineering techniques.

Customer Service Chatbot

This application involves the development of an advanced, multilingual customer service chatbot. Utilizing OpenAI's GPT-3 for natural language processing, the chatbot is designed to handle various customer queries with different personalities and in multiple languages. The application is structured to be adaptable, allowing for the integration of additional contexts and features.

Customer Service Chatbot involves persona pattern and zero-shot prompting technique. This is the basic form of prompt engineering technique to contextualize the GPT-3's knowledge.
